// conichi SDK

conichi-SDK is a tool that allows users to be recognised around the Beacon through Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE). The SDK is the easiest way to integrate the conichi functionalities into any third party iOS or Android App.

// How does the conichi-SDK work?
